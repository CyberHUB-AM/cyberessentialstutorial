Introduction

Welcome to the Cyber Essentials tutorial. This tutorial will guide you through the basic concepts and requirements for securing your IT infrastructure.

1. Firewalls

Firewalls are essential for protecting your network by controlling the incoming and outgoing traffic based on predetermined security rules. They help prevent unauthorized access to your network.

2. Secure Configuration

Ensuring that your devices and software are configured securely is crucial. This includes removing unnecessary software, changing default passwords, and disabling auto-run features.
